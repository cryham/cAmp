    Crystal AMP (cAmp)

    Windows Audio Player with custom playlist.

    Key features

    • Plays most common audio files: WAV,FLAC,OGG,MP3,MP2,MPC,APE,WV,WMA
    • Large visualization (types: FFT spectrum, oscilloscope, spectrogram)
    • Global hot keys
    • Song rating, rating filtering in playlist
    • Playlist tabs (configurable rows and columns)
    • Bookmarks (3 levels)
